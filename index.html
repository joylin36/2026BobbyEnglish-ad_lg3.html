📝 實用聽說練習精華 | Practical Conversations

  • 能流暢詢問並準確指引社區中不同場所與位置的方向。[cite: 3]
    Fluently ask for and give directions to various places within the neighborhood.
  • 自信地描述自己或他人正在穿著的衣物、顏色以及款式穿搭。[cite: 3]
    Confidently describe clothing items, colors, and dressing styles of oneself and others.
  • 熟練詢問他人的計畫，並分享自己最喜歡的休閒娛樂與去過的地點。[cite: 3]
    Master asking about plans and sharing leisure activities and places visited.
  • 大方地表達並分配日常生活中的家務分工，學會責任與合作表達。[cite: 3]
    Express and delegate everyday household chores, learning responsibility and teamwork terms.
  • 流利說出十二個月份與四季的天氣變化，並描述不同季節盛行的活動。[cite: 3]
    Fluently name the 12 months and four seasons, describing popular seasonal activities.
  • 學會運用過去式動詞，精準分享昨天或上週末發生的精彩故事。[cite: 3]
    Learn to use past tense verbs to accurately share wonderful stories from yesterday or last weekend.
  • 生動探討各種交通工具，並能夠規劃與詢問一段有趣的旅行冒險。[cite: 3]
    Vividly discuss modes of transportation and plan/ask about fun travel adventures.
  • 自信分享未來的理想職業，並能描述不同工作在社會中的貢獻。[cite: 3]
    Confidently share dream occupations and describe the contributions of various professions.

官方認證核心語法 | Grammar Essentials

精準解鎖中階時態：熟練現在進行式之進階雙向問答、初階動詞過去式變化（規則與不規則動詞變化）、方向介系詞（Next to / Across from）、代名詞所有格，以及問句 Whom/Whose 的靈活應用。[cite: 3]

Unlock intermediate tenses: proficient in advanced Present Continuous forms, Past Simple tense rules, prepositions of direction, possessive pronouns, and Wh-questions.
