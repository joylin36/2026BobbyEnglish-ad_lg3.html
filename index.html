💡 課程理念 | Course Concept

從「愛開口」邁向「有邏輯」！Let's Go 3 正式帶領孩子跨越純口語遊戲，啟動系統化結構。

本課程將帶領孩子跨越初階的純口語對話，全面啟動英語閱讀理解、系統化文法結構與精準的單句書寫。巴比老師運用嚴謹而不嚴肅的專業引導，讓孩子自然地將零散單字拼湊成完整且符合邏輯的想法，達到大膽說、自信寫的嶄新里程碑！

Moving from fun conversations to structured language! Let's Go 3 introduces foundational reading comprehension, essential grammar rules, and basic sentence writing.

This course goes beyond basic oral communication, fully activating reading skills and grammatical frameworks. Through professional guidance, we help kids confidently translate their thoughts into correctly written and spoken English with independence and joy!

📝 系統化文法基礎精華 | Grammar Essentials

  • 會詢問他人擁有或未擁有的物品、表達自己擁有的物品。
    Ask and answer questions about possessions and express what one owns.
  • 詢問他人穿著的衣物及衣物的所有者。
    Ask about clothing and ownership.
  • 詢問某人正往何處去及所搭乘的交通工具。
    Ask about destinations and transportation.
  • 詢問職業、並簡述其工作內容。
    Ask about occupations and describe job duties.
  • 會說出星期以及課程名稱。
    State days of the week and school subjects.
  • 會運用頻率副詞、會說出較細的時間。
    Use adverbs of frequency and tell exact time.
  • 會運用過去式結構並詢問他人正在做的事情。
    Use past tense structures and ask about current actions.
  • 核心語法架構 (Grammar Foundations)：名詞所有格、現在進行式、Which引導的選擇問句、have現在式問答、Be動詞過去式、規則與不規則動詞過去式、助動詞did、連接詞 but、時間介系詞與頻率副詞。
    Master possessive nouns, present progressive, "Which" questions, "have" structures, past tense (Be-verbs, regular/irregular verbs, auxiliary "did"), conjunction "but", prepositions of time, and adverbs of frequency.
